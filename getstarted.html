Why Cybersecurity is Important?

Cybersecurity is essential in today's digital age. It protects your data, privacy, and online presence from a wide range of threats, including hackers, viruses, and data breaches. Without strong cybersecurity measures, you are vulnerable to financial loss, identity theft, and damage to your reputation. By staying informed and regularly testing your cybersecurity knowledge, you can better defend yourself and your assets in the online world.
